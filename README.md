# LWS VS Days Optimizer

Weekly Alliance Duel (VS Days) point calculator + strategic guide for Last War: Survival players.

**Live:** https://bullochman.github.io/lws-vs-days-static/

## What it does

Tells you exactly what to do TODAY to score the maximum VS Days points for your alliance's weekly Duel.

- **Today banner** — auto-detects the current day, shows the theme, tip, and available VP
- **6-day grid** — click any day to see its scoring actions + strategy tip
- **Point calculator** — enter what you've completed today, see estimated score
- **Individual chest tracker** — Basic (540k) / Advanced (2.3M) / Super (7.2M) progress bars
- **Strategic rules panel** — every-week principles from the LWS KB

## Grounded in the KB

Every scoring value + strategic rule is sourced from `LWS_Knowledge_Base` §08 (Alliance Systems) and §09 (Events). No made-up numbers.

Key facts:
- First alliance to 7 VP wins the week (13 VP total pool)
- Saturday (Enemy Buster) alone = 4 VP — biggest swing
- Friday (Total Mobilization) has 4× training-point multiplier
- Saturday has 5× multiplier vs. paired rival alliance troops
