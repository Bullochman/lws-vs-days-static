Every-week strategic rules KB §08 · §09

  • Saturday alone = 4 VP — the single biggest swing of the week. Win it plus any 3 of Days 2-5 and you flip a losing week.
  • Points score on completion, not on speedup use. Start the queue BEFORE the day flips; dump speedups to finish it inside the window.
  • First 5 minutes after daily reset score nothing — wait for the event-start banner before firing speedups.
  • Arms Race overlap — when the VS Day theme matches the active Arms Race phase, the same action scores in both events. (Tuesday Base Expansion × City Building phase, Friday Total Mobilization × Unit Progression phase.)
  • Friday's 4× training multiplier is the biggest weekly efficiency spike. Save all training speedups from Days 1-4 for Friday.
  • Saturday Enemy Buster: 5× multiplier vs. your paired rival alliance. Kill their troops, not just anyone's.
Facts sourced from the LWS Knowledge Base (§08 Alliance Systems · §09 Events).
